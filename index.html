Executive Summary

After 12 remarkable years of impacting thousands of students across India, the Middle East, and the US, Learning Integration for Education is launching iLife - The World's First AI School (KG to PG), powered by our Eduzon.ai platform.

This document outlines our comprehensive 5-month execution roadmap from January 2026 launch through May 20, 2026.

Our Vision

iLife AI School reimagines education from the ground up - not just adding technology to education, but creating a comprehensive AI-integrated school spanning kindergarten through post-graduation.

Core Differentiators:

  • Social-Emotional Learning First - Building human connections in an AI world
  • AI Literacy from Early Ages - Teaching productive vs disruptive technology use
  • Comprehensive Coverage - KG to PG (Post-Graduation)
  • Proven Platform - Eduzon.ai with 12 years of educational excellence

12-Year Foundation (2013-2025)

Our journey has established:

  • Foundational literacy programs across multiple markets
  • Pioneered STEM education initiatives
  • Transformed early childhood learning
  • Built trust with countless schools and families
  • Developed the Eduzon.ai platform
  • Established presence in India, Middle East, and United States
